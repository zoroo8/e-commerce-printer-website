Printer Detail


The Canon imageCLASS LBP6030w is an easy to use, wireless, single function laser printer that is an ideal solution for a home or small office environment. The compact, space saving design delivers professional quality output in one small footprint. The LBP6030w delivers exceptional black and white laser output at speeds of up to 19 pages-per-minute, with your first print in your hands in 8 seconds or less. Wireless connectivity allows you to print from almost anywhere in your home or office.
