How technology has affected in Daily life?

Introduction
In the future world of 2024, most aspects of life are transformed by technology that affect efficiency in transportation, security and safety; availability of food resources, their methods of socialization. It has made learning a better experience, it has given access to bodies of information previously unavailable and also allowed global communities to develop in the online world. Although the implementation of technology has made our lives more convenient, enabled ideas and resources to be shared and fostered an environment for research, some technologies have had negative effects concerning mental health, even social polarization extending towards the privacy violations. The intensification of the spread of AI tools, such as ChatGPT and other digital technologies made by scientists even more critical questions about playing technology into our daily lives.

In context of Daily Life:
1. Communication:
- Instant Communication: The increasing penetration rate of smartphones and messaging apps have enabled people to communicate in an instantry, regardless of their locations.
- Social Media: The manner in which customers communicate, socialize, and learn about affairs concerts today seems to have been drastically altered with social media sites such as Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.
2. Information Access:
- Internet: The availability of vast amounts of information with internet access to it has made all such things possible. Information is freely available baby video sharing publicly and privately regarding social engagement, sports, politics, develop diseases and cure.
- Search Engines: The advent of search engines such as Google has changed the way we find and receive information today by answering questions and solving problems in a less complicated manner.
3. Entertainment:
- Streaming Services: Services like Netflix, Hulu, and Spotify have changed the way we consume entertainment, offering on-demand access to movies, TV shows, and music.
- Gaming: Video games, both online and offline, have become a significant form of entertainment, connecting people globally in virtual environments.
4. Health and Fitness:
- Wearable Technology: Devices like fitness trackers and smartwatches help individuals monitor their health, track physical activity, and stay motivated to maintain a healthy lifestyle.
- Telemedicine: Technology has facilitated remote medical consultations, making healthcare more accessible and convenient.
5. Transportation:
- Ride-Sharing: Services like Patho and Indrive have transformed transportation, providing convenient alternatives to traditional taxis and public transportations.
- Navigation Apps: GPS and navigation apps have made it easier for people to navigate unfamiliar areas mainly with Google maps.

Secondly, on Business:
1. Communication and Collaboration:
- Video Conferencing: Already, online platforms such as Zoom and Microsoft Teams have become great applications for the innovation of remote work, and allow virtual sessions to be conducted and enabling the members all over the globe via technology.
- Email and Messaging: Electronic communication has revolutionized business relations between companies, thus streamlining the work on correspondence which is now faster and more efficient to work with.
2. Automation and Efficiency:
- Workflow Automation: Automation is a process of reducing or eliminating human engagement by replacing humans through software and applications; business use it to replace repetitive tasks with machine that have no errors while working on.
- AI and Machine Learning: All these technologies allow to analyze data, and the improvement of processes contributing to more rational decision-making.
3. E-Commerce:
- Online Shopping: E-commerce platforms like Daraz have been an active E-commerce website, providing a convenient way for consumers to shop from home or any place.
- Digital Payments: Electronic payment methods, including credit cards and mobile wallets like Esewa, Khalti, IME pay; have become best for transactions, simplifying transactions.
4. Data Analytics:
- Big Data: Organizations uses big data analytics to gain idea of what customers do when making their purchases into knowledge; they make the right moves.
- Business Intelligence: Business intelligence tools allows organizations to optimize decision-making processes based on their reduced outlook, thus providing eligible information while creating usable insight from the crude data.
5. Globalization:
- Remote Work: The employees can work in remote location for the technology enhancement, and this creates an opportunity for hiring global talent without having constant office access.
- Global Markets: The availability of such products as an audience market becomes different over the globe and businesses can also reach globally through your online marketing adopting international e-commerce.

Negative Impacts of Technology:
Although there are a lot of positive feature resulting from technological development, it have negative impacts. Social media has been societal curse because it affect communication and changed the ideas people held about social life. However, people have become more anti-social after partaking in various social gatherings taking into account difference in the forms of socializing. Today, to create a relationship with another one does not necessarily need to be physically involved. For instance, with advent of video games, activities which promoted the health of young people have been substituted. The current generation of children tends to spend much time in front of televisions and on computer games while leaving absolute no time for outdoor activities. This has promoted prevalence of such like habits in eating which lead to diseases.

Conclusion
Moreover, technology never takes rest, and you can bet that it won't be long before some of the devices and services we've covered here are taken over in many cases, their next iteration is already being worked on in a lab somewhere. Regardless, there's no denying that technology has, and will continue to, have a huge impact on our lives, in one way or any another way.
